PTO-01 — Paid Time Off (PTO) Policies (Vacation + Personal Leave + Holidays)
System
BambooHR (www.aap.bamboohr.com)
Owner
HR Department
When
Time is put in the day after it’s used
Output
Approved time off requests in BambooHR; PTO balances maintained; holiday schedule applied consistently
Key Points for Employees:
Confirm they meet eligibility requirements for the PTO type they are requesting (Vacation, Personal Long Term Sick Leave, or Floating Holiday).
PTO is not allowed to be taken before it is accrued.
For planned time off, requests are to be submitted no later than 5:00 pm the day before time off is needed so staffing can be arranged.
For unexpected personal leave, notify your supervisor before your scheduled start time whenever possible (and on each additional day of absence).
If requesting more than five (5) consecutive vacation days, written approval by the Company President is required.
Steps
Step
Action
1
Employee reviews PTO balance and availability in BambooHR.
2
Employee submits a Time Off request in BambooHR, selecting the correct PTO type and entering dates and hours.
3
Supervisor reviews the request in BambooHR and approves or denies based on business needs and staffing.
4
For unexpected personal leave, employee notifies the supervisor as soon as possible; the request is entered/updated in BambooHR when confirmed.
5
If the employee is required to work a designated/observed holiday, the supervisor coordinates a Floating Holiday to be used within 90 days.
6
Employee updates or cancels the BambooHR request if plans change and notifies the supervisor as soon as possible.

Personal Leave (all-purpose PTO for vacation, illness/injury, and personal business)
Regular full-time employees: 3 personal days (24 hours) upon initial eligibility; 4 personal days (32 hours) each year after 1 year of eligible service; 5 personal days (40 hours) each year after 5 years of eligible service.
Part-time employees: 1 hour per 30 hours worked, up to 24 hours upon initial eligibility; 32 hours/year after 1 year of eligible service; 40 hours/year after 5 years of eligible service.
Personal leave has a 60-day waiting period before it can be used; personal leave may be requested in one-hour increments.
Unused personal leave is forfeited at the end of the benefit year unless state law requires carryover; unused personal leave is not paid out at termination.

Observed holidays: Holidays that fall on a Saturday are observed on Friday; holidays that fall on a Sunday are observed on Monday.
If an employee is required to work on a designated/observed holiday, the employee receives a floating holiday to be used within 90 days after the holiday.
Checks:
Correct PTO type entered into Payclock; required approvals obtained for extended vacation; holiday observation and floating holiday rules applied consistently in each department.
Escalate to HR Department if:
Time off is disputed or unclear; a request conflicts with eligibility or accrual rules; PTO balances appear incorrect; an exception is needed due to operational staffing; or there are questions about holiday eligibility, observation, or floating holiday use.
