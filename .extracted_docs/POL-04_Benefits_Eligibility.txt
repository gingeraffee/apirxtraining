All Employees — Benefits (Eligibility & Enrollment)
BEN-01 — Benefit Eligibility & Enrollment (Timing, Options, and Key Rules)
System
HR / Benefits Administration
Owner
HR Manager
When
At hire, during eligibility milestones, and during enrollment windows
Output
Correct benefits activated/enrolled; eligibility dates tracked
Before you start
Eligibility timing differs by benefit and by employee classification (full-time vs. part-time).
Some benefits are free and available to all employees; others require enrollment when the employee becomes eligible.
If an employee believes eligibility dates are incorrect, escalate to HR for review and correction.
Benefit eligibility timeline (by milestone)
Milestone
Who is eligible
Benefits / notes
Day 1
All employees
LinkedIn Learning, Employee Assistance Program (EAP), AAP Perks (free).
1st of the month after hire
All employees
Teladoc (free).
60 days
All employees / Full-Time employees
All employees become eligible to use Personal Time Off. Full-Time employees also become eligible to use Vacation Time.
1st of the month after 60 days
Full-Time employees
Medical, Dental, Vision, 401(k), and supplemental coverages become available for enrollment. These are deducted from their paycheck biweekly.
1 year from hire date (and 1,000 hours worked; age 21+)
Part-Time employees
401(k) part-time eligibility begins only after BOTH 1 year of employment AND 1,000 hours worked are met. Employee must be at least 21 years old to enroll.
4 years from hire date, then every 5 years after
Full-Time employees
Long Term Sick Leave eligibility: 80 hours at 4-year anniversary; then every 5 year. Bank limit: 180 hours. Use requires 3 consecutive days or more out and a doctor’s note.
Every 5 years starting at 5th anniversary
All employees
Service Awards eligibility (service award issued every 5 years starting on the 5th anniversary).
Benefit details (plan options and key information)
Medical: two plans available — PPO or HDHP with HSA.
Dental: two plans available — Base Plan or Buy Up Plan.
Vision and Dental are through Guardian and do not issue insurance cards. Coverage information is available in the Guardian Anytime app.
401(k) includes a company match: AAP matches 100% of the first 3% an employee contributes, and 50% of the next 2% of employee contribution (i.e., employee contributes 5% and AAP matches 4%).
Steps
Step
Action
1
At hire, HR provides the employee with benefit availability guidance and access instructions for any Day 1 benefits.
2
HR confirms employee classification (Full-Time vs. Part-Time) and enters HR Tasks for Benefit Enrollment.
3
On the 1st of the month after hire, HR confirms Teladoc eligibility is active and provides access instructions if needed.
4
At 60 days, HR verifies the time off policies are correct. The employee can use Personal Time Off; for Full-Time employees, confirm Vacation Time availability.
5
On the 1st of the month after 60 days (Full-Time only), HR sends enrollment instructions and confirms enrollment window and required elections (Medical/Dental/Vision/401(k)/supplemental).
6
For Part-Time employees, HR monitors service time and hours worked; once BOTH 1 year of employment AND 1,000 hours worked are met (and employee is age 21+), HR provides 401(k) enrollment instructions.
7
For Full-Time employees approaching Long Term Sick Leave milestones (4 years; then every 5 years), HR confirms balance and communicates usage rules (3 consecutive days+ and doctor’s note).
8
HR tracks service anniversaries for Service Awards and coordinates award issuance every 5 years starting at the 5th anniversary.
Checks
Eligibility milestones calculated correctly from hire date; employee classification confirmed; free benefits activated appropriately; full-time enrollment offered at 1st of month after 60 days; part-time 401(k) eligibility verified (1 year + 1,000 hours + age 21+); Guardian Anytime guidance provided (no cards for dental/vision); 401(k) match communicated accurately; Long Term Sick Leave bank/usage rules applied; Service Awards tracked every 5 years starting at 5th anniversary.
Escalate to HR Manager / Benefits Administrator if
Employee disputes eligibility date; employee classification or hours worked are unclear; enrollment access issues occur; coverage options need clarification; Long Term Sick Leave usage is requested; or any exception to standard eligibility timing is requested.
